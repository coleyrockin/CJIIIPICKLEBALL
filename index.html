Our Mission

Why CJ's Pickleball?

CJ's Pickleball was born out of pure passion for the fastest-growing sport in the world. Our mission is simple: bring everyone together to love pickleball worldwide.

Whether you're a seasoned pro, a weekend warrior, or picking up a paddle for the very first time, you belong here. We share tips, exclusive gear discounts, tournament info, and most importantly — a community that celebrates the game and the friendships it creates.
